University's Events

Kabaddi

At Atmiya University, the spirit of unity and competition comes alive with the annual Kabaddi tournament that brings together students from various university departments. This thrilling event fosters camaraderie and sportsmanship as participants showcase their skills and teamwork on the Kabaddi mat. With enthusiastic cheers from the sidelines, these departmental clashes create an electrifying atmosphere on campus. Students engage in intense battles, demonstrating agility, strength, and strategic thinking. The Kabaddi tournament not only promotes physical fitness but also strengthens the bonds among departments, making it a highlight of the university's sporting calendar.
